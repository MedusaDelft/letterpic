Custom settings

        Yoy could define custom color scheme, font color and family, and relative font size.
        $(".userpic-letter-custom").letterpic({
            colors: ["#000", "#111", "#222", "#333" ],
            font: "Tahoma",
            fontColor: "#94090D",
            fontSize: 0.3
        });
